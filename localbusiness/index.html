Dick's Drive-In is a well-known burger fast food in Seattle. Dick's has branches in both Wallingford and Downtown Seattle. There are no seats in the Wallingford's Dick's, but there are seats in another branch in Downtown Seattle. Only burgers, fries, drinks, shakes and ice cream are sold in-store. The price is quite cheap. Dick's Drive-In is well known in Seattle and almost can be a local landmark.
