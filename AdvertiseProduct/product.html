OnePlus6 Overview

"Special" OnePlus 6

In the first half of 2018, and it was squeezed into the wave of "Notch Display" phones. For people that have always paid attention to the visual perception, "Notch Display" is currently a safer choice by maintaining a larger screen size.

Using the word "special" to evaluate the design of this year's OnePlus 6 seems to be appropriate. After all, OnePlus 6 is a new flagship that uses new materials and has a big difference between the design and the previous generation. Of course, you can still say that the design such as "Notch Display" and double-sided glass is following the trend, and it can be considered that its appearance is not outstanding and innovative enough.
